What We Do

Premium Services

Skimming & Reskimming

Transform old walls with a mirror-smooth finish. We specialize in skimming over old plaster and plasterboard for perfect results every time.

Dry Lining & Insulation

Professional dry lining services to improve your home's thermal efficiency. Expert installation of insulated plasterboard.

Rendering & Pebble Dashing

Durable external finishes including traditional sand & cement rendering and classic pebble dashing for weather protection.

Repair & Restoration

Invisible repairs for cracks, water damage, and ceilings. From patch repairs to period cornice restoration.

Lime Plastering

Traditional breathable lime plastering for heritage homes. Preserving the integrity of older buildings with authentic techniques.

New Builds & Renovations

Complete plastering packages for new constructions and extensions. Fast, reliable turnaround for jobs of any size.

Who We Are

The Standard in Quality Plastering

Welcome to Raymond Murtagh Plastering. We are a family-run business with over 35 years of experience in the trade. Starting from the ground up, we have built a reputation for reliability, speed, and uncompromising quality.

We understand that inviting tradesmen into your home requires trust. That's why we treat every property with the utmost respect. We use protective cover sheets for all work areas and guarantee to leave your home clean and tidy. No job is too small, and every quote is free.
